Melrose Events

The small town of Melrose is home to a surprising number of events throughout the year.

Check out the information below about recurring events, festivals, and other special events.

  • Home
  • Events

Recurring Events

Many community events, both small and large, are hosted in Melrose on a weekly or monthly basis.

To learn more, visit the Melrose Business and Community Association's calendar, scroll down, or click here.

Festivals

Melrose is home to two annual festivals, the Butterfly Festival in the spring months and the Arts and Crafts Festival in the winter months.

Check back here later to see those dates and more information.

Special Events

In addition to annual festivals and recurring events, Melrose is home to many other special events.

Check back another time to see more information about a particular event that is coming up.
